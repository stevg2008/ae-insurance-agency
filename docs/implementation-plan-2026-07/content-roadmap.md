## Prioritization Framework

Each content piece is scored against:
1. **GSC demand signal** — Do we already have impressions proving search demand exists?
2. **Topical gap** — Does a competitor or established source cover this and we don't?
3. **User intent** — Does this serve a genuine question a 64+ Medicare beneficiary asks?
4. **Internal linking** — Does this fill a gap in our existing content cluster?

All 10 pieces must pass the test: "Would a $20,000/month SEO agency recommend this in 2026?"
